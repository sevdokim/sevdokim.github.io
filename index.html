About Me

I am a particle physics researcher with over 10 years of experience in experimental high-energy physics. My work focuses on studying hadronic interactions and developing detector systems for large-scale experiments at CERN and IHEP.

Since 2011, I have been actively involved in the Hyperon-M experiment at IHEP and the ALICE experiment at CERN's LHC, contributing to detector development, data analysis, and physics results publication.

My research interests include partial wave analysis, Monte Carlo simulations of particle interactions, and studies of neutral states produced in meson-nucleus interactions.
